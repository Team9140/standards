# Team 9140 battery construction

# Batteries are to be constructed with the following materials:
- MK battery ES17-12 [SLA battery](https://www.revrobotics.com/rev-19-2487/)
- 4 AWG copper compression lugs [6926K309](https://www.mcmaster.com/6926K309/)
- 4 AWG welding [cable](https://www.windynation.com/products/4-gauge-black-red-welding-battery-cable-various-lengths-available)
- 6 AWG red SB50 [connectors](https://powerwerx.com/anderson-sb-connectors-sb50-50amp)
- adhesive linked heat shrink in red [7270K22](https://www.mcmaster.com/7270K22/) and black [7270K82](https://www.mcmaster.com/7270K82/)
- clear heat shrink
- 0.625" 10-32 bolt [91251A344](https://www.mcmaster.com/91251A344/)
- 10-32 nylon locknut [91831A511](https://www.mcmaster.com/91831A511/)

# The following tools are required:
- hydraulic crimper https://powerwerx.com/hyd-2-hydraulic-crimping-tool
- heat gun
- large wire cutter
- knife
