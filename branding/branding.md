# Branding

## Team Name
The name of the team is "The Robobots". In written form the team may be referred to as follows:
- The Robobots
- Team 9140
- 9140
- Team 9140: The Robobots

The team may referred to in any way not included above. Incorrect examples are as follows:
- The RoboBots
- RoboBots
- The Robo Bots
- Robo Bots
- robobots
